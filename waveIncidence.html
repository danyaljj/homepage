Wave incidence at a three-layered surface

Smith Chart Interactive Simulator

Download: [MATLAB GUI]

Demo: YouTube

A button is added for separating the “incident” and “reflecting” waves. Two sliders are laid at the bottom, for changing the width of two layers at right side. Thus, by changing the values of sliders, one can find the condition of matching between layers.

To start the GUI:

>>  WaveGUI_ver2

To exit the progam:

>> Ctrl+C
