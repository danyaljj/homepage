Art

As a strange natural force, I was attracted to calligraphy when I was just 12 years old. I was practicing times and times, hours and hours during days without any exhaustion. After a while I got a lot of advances in Persian Calligraphy (Nastaliq) and Arabic Calligraphy (Nashkh and Sols). Regarding to the fact that I was just working for my own satisfaction, I wasn't so serious about participating in any official calligraphy tournaments. The only awards I got are best act in Nastaliq and Naskh between urban students during years 2004 and 2005. Now I sometimes spend time for this art. It's good to get official degree in Calligraphy!
Along Calligraphy I started to test my ability in painting. I tested several kinds of instruments e.g. pastel, oil painting, water-color painting, etc. I discovered my huge abilities in imagination. Synthesizing engineering with beauty and feelings! Unfortunately because of my education, I surrendered this beautiful art for while, nevertheless deep in my heart I have hope to have the chance to continue that again.
